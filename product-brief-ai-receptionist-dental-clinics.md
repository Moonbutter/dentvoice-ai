# Product Brief: AI Receptionist for Dental Clinics

## Product Name Options

- DentVoice AI
- SmileDesk AI
- ClinicCall AI
- DentaAssist
- VoiceDent AI

Recommended working name: **DentVoice AI**

## 1. Overview

DentVoice AI is an AI-powered virtual receptionist built for dental clinics. It answers patient phone calls, handles common front-desk questions, captures appointment requests, detects urgent cases, and sends confirmations through WhatsApp.

The product is designed to function like a human receptionist, but with 24/7 availability, instant response times, and structured digital follow-up for every call.

## 2. Problem Statement

Dental clinics in India often rely heavily on manual front-desk operations. This creates operational gaps that directly impact revenue and patient experience.

Common pain points include:

- Missed patient calls while staff are busy during procedures
- High receptionist workload from repetitive queries
- Lost appointments due to delayed or unanswered responses
- No after-hours coverage for inbound patient calls
- Rising front-desk staffing and operational costs

Many inbound calls are repetitive and do not require a trained human to handle them, including:

- Appointment booking
- Rescheduling or cancellation requests
- Clinic timings
- Location and directions
- Consultation availability
- Treatment-related inquiries

## 3. Product Vision

Create the default AI front desk for Indian clinics, starting with dental practices, by automating voice-based patient interactions and converting every inbound call into an actionable outcome.

## 5. Why This Fits the Indian Market

India is a strong fit for this product because clinic operations are still highly manual and communication behavior is heavily voice- and WhatsApp-driven.

Market fit signals:

- Patients frequently prefer calling over filling forms
- WhatsApp is widely used for business communication
- Small clinics often have limited front-desk bandwidth
- Appointment workflows are still managed informally in many clinics

This creates a large opportunity for a specialized AI receptionist product that feels local, practical, and immediately valuable.

## 7. Product Description

DentVoice AI is a voice AI receptionist that automatically answers incoming clinic calls and handles common patient interactions in natural language.

It can:

- Greet callers professionally
- Understand patient intent
- Answer common clinic questions
- Capture appointment details
- Identify urgent cases
- Send WhatsApp confirmations
- Update the clinic dashboard with summaries and next actions

## 8. How the Product Works

### Step 1: Patient Calls the Clinic

The patient calls the clinic’s phone number.

### Step 2: AI Answers Automatically

The AI receptionist answers the call in a natural, human-like tone.

Example:

> "Hello, thank you for calling Smile Dental Clinic. How may I assist you today?"

### Step 3: AI Detects Intent

The system understands why the patient is calling, such as:

- Booking an appointment
- Asking about treatment availability
- Requesting pricing information
- Rescheduling an appointment
- Asking for clinic directions
- Reporting a possible dental emergency

### Step 4: AI Collects Relevant Information

The AI gathers the right details based on the caller’s intent, such as:

- Patient name
- Phone number
- Preferred date and time
- Reason for visit
- Treatment interest

### Step 5: AI Sends Confirmation

After the call, the patient receives a WhatsApp message with:

- Appointment request confirmation
- Clinic address
- Contact details
- Relevant follow-up information

### Step 6: Clinic Dashboard Updates

Clinic staff can view:

- New appointment requests
- Missed revenue opportunities
- Emergency flags
- Patient summaries
- Call outcomes

## 10. User Flow Summary

1. Patient calls clinic
2. AI greets and understands request
3. AI answers questions or collects booking details
4. AI sends WhatsApp confirmation
5. Clinic team reviews the lead or appointment request in dashboard
6. Staff follows up where needed

### MVP Capabilities

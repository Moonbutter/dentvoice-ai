# DentVoice AI Subscription Pricing Playbook

## Core Positioning

DentVoice AI is not just an AI receptionist.

It is a revenue recovery product for appointment-led and inquiry-led businesses.

The core promise is simple:

- answer inbound calls instantly
- capture missed demand
- convert missed calls into appointments or qualified leads
- reduce revenue leakage caused by slow or missed responses

## The Problem

Businesses spend money on:

- Google Ads
- Meta Ads
- SEO
- referrals

Then they lose customers because nobody answered the phone in time.

That is the actual pain.

## The Product Pitch

Use this message:

> Businesses usually do not lose customers because of bad service. They lose them because no one answered in time.

DentVoice AI helps service businesses stop losing inbound revenue by answering instantly, capturing lead details, booking appointments, and keeping the follow-up workflow visible inside one dashboard.

## Pricing Model

### Starter

- Price: `₹4,999/month`
- Best for: one clinic or one business location
- Includes:
  - AI call answering workflow
  - appointment capture
  - dashboard and inbox
  - reminders and analytics
  - missed-lead recovery

### Growth

- Price: `₹9,999/month`
- Best for: busy clinics and small multi-user teams
- Includes:
  - everything in Starter
  - higher call volume support
  - multi-user workflow access
  - stronger analytics
  - branding control

### Multi-Location

- Price: `₹14,999+/month`
- Best for: chains and businesses with multiple branches
- Includes:
  - everything in Growth
  - branch-level operations
  - role-based workflow
  - export-ready reporting
  - multi-location oversight

## Payment Options

Start simple:

- monthly billing
- quarterly billing
- UPI
- bank transfer
- invoice with card payment

Recommended early setup for India:

- UPI for fast collections
- bank transfer for clinics that prefer direct payment
- quarterly billing option for customers who want a small commitment discount

## Why `₹5,000/month` Works

The pricing is easy to justify when the business already loses revenue from missed calls.

### Dental Clinics

- one missed patient can be worth `₹2,000` to `₹20,000+`
- procedures, implants, aligners, cosmetic work, and repeat treatment increase lifetime value

### Dermatology and Cosmetic Clinics

- one converted lead can be worth `₹20,000` to `₹1,00,000+`
- inquiries are high value and often time-sensitive

### Real Estate

- one serious lead can justify months of subscription fees
- speed to first response matters a lot

### Salons

- lower ticket size, but high repeat behavior
- missed weekend bookings still create measurable leakage

The simplest ROI line:

> If DentVoice helps recover even 2 or 3 missed opportunities in a month, the subscription can pay for itself.

## Ideal Customer Rollout

### Tier 1 - Start Here

These are the strongest early customers.

#### Dental Clinics

Why this is the best starting point:

- appointment-based workflow
- small receptionist teams
- high treatment value
- direct fit with the current product

Target examples:

- `1-5 chair dental clinics`
- cosmetic dentistry clinics
- implant specialists

#### Dermatology and Cosmetic Clinics

Examples:

- skin clinics
- hair transplant clinics
- aesthetic clinics

Why they are strong:

- high-value treatments
- lots of inquiry volume through calls and WhatsApp
- many operate beyond regular business hours

#### Physiotherapy Clinics

Why they fit:

- appointment-heavy workflow
- repeat visits
- small team size

### Tier 2 - After Validation

#### Real Estate Brokers and Agencies

Why this is attractive:

- large lead value
- heavy dependence on ad-driven calls
- brokers often cannot answer immediately

DentVoice use case:

- answer calls
- qualify budget and location
- capture lead details
- book site visits

#### Automobile Dealerships

Use cases:

- test-drive booking
- lead capture
- basic query handling

#### Coaching Institutes

Examples:

- JEE / NEET coaching
- IELTS / PTE institutes
- skill training centers

Why they fit:

- large inquiry volume
- missed follow-ups are common

### Tier 3 - Scale Later

#### Salons and Spas

- large market

- lower average ticket size

#### Gyms and Fitness Centers

Use cases:

- membership inquiries
- trial booking

#### Restaurants

- lower willingness to pay
- better as a later expansion category

## Recommended Sales Angle By Vertical

### Dental

Pitch:

> Dental clinics lose appointments when calls are missed during procedures, lunch hours, or after clinic hours. DentVoice answers instantly, captures the patient, and reduces front-desk revenue leakage.

### Dermatology and Cosmetic

Pitch:

> Cosmetic and skin clinics handle high-value inquiries where one missed call can mean a lost treatment worth tens of thousands of rupees. DentVoice helps capture those leads before they move elsewhere.

### Real Estate

Pitch:

> Real estate businesses lose high-value leads because agents cannot answer instantly while driving or showing property. DentVoice captures the inquiry and keeps it inside the follow-up workflow.

### Salons

Pitch:

> Salons lose peak-time bookings when appointment calls go unanswered. DentVoice helps convert those calls into scheduled bookings and repeat customers.

## Suggested Sales Script

Use this structure:

1. Start with the pain
   - "How many calls do you think your clinic misses every week when the receptionist is busy or the clinic is closed?"
2. Connect pain to revenue
   - "If even a few of those callers book elsewhere, that is lost revenue from patients you already had the chance to capture."
3. Introduce DentVoice
   - "DentVoice answers instantly, captures the inquiry, and keeps the lead inside one follow-up workflow."
4. Justify pricing
   - "At `₹4,999/month`, the product can pay for itself if it recovers even a small number of missed opportunities."

## Best First Go-To-Market Strategy

Do not try to sell every industry first.

Start here:

1. dental clinics

3. physiotherapy clinics

Then expand after validation:

4. real estate

6. coaching institutes

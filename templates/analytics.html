Revenue Recovery Story

Translate appointment workflow into a business outcome a clinic owner understands

  • Estimated revenue recoveredUses the clinic’s average booking value multiplied by active non-cancelled appointments.
  • Revenue realizedShows the value already converted into completed appointments.
  • Pipeline at riskHot inbound calls still need fast follow-up to avoid leakage.

What These Charts Mean

A quick learning layer for understanding the analytics

  • Appointments by StatusShows whether bookings are getting confirmed and completed instead of staying pending.
  • Source and IntentHelps you understand where demand comes from and what patients usually call about.
  • Lead ScoresHighlights which calls deserve fast follow-up from the clinic team.
  • 7-Day TrendGives a lightweight activity graph even before adding any paid analytics stack.
  • Revenue MetricsHelp explain the ROI of the product without needing paid telecom or AI integrations yet.
